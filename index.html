About Digiset

Digiset was created by four students who believed you should always be in the know regarding your assets. Frustrated with not knowing where your money is going or not how much you really have, the Digiset team set out to design a unique app that mitigates your financial unawareness.

Through our initial research, we were able to identify that most people are not fully aware of everything they regularly spend money on nor do they know what the items they own are worth.

With the assistance of advanced databases and powerful API's, we were able to develop an app that can actively track a multitude of assets in real-time. With our carefully designed categorization system, our users can effortlessly find relevant and actionable information regarding their assets.

The future is bright for Digiset. We regularly add new asset categories to track and we plan to add a state-of-the-art marketplace sometime in the near future so that our users can profit off the various analytics and insights we provide them.
